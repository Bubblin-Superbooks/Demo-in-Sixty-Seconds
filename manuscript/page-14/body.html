Follow your hand

When it comes to sketching and coloring the best way to go forward is to follow the hand. It is very unlikely that you (and I) as first-timers will be able to draw anything systematically. We can't produce a scene imagined in our heads. We're not trained for that.

Worry not! Goof around as much as you can until you're comfortable with a style of your own. In fact the more fun you have with it, the better it gets. Do not force yourself along a rigid storyline because the drawings will not work out that way.

Strange as it may sound, our hands automatically led us to the current story of our animation. We had a different version of it when we started out but then the characters took form and forced us away from the original score.

It now feels as if the hand had its own story to tell.
