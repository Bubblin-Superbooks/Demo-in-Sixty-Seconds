Follow your hand

When it comes to sketching and drawing, the best way to do it is to follow your hand. It's unlikely that as first-timers you (or I) would be able to draw systematically and produce a scene as imagined in the head. We're not trained for that. But don't worry about it. Goof around a bit.

In fact the more you have fun with it, the better it gets. Do not dig yourself too deep with a story that you'd planned for. It may not work out that way.

Strange as it may sound, our hands automatically led us to the current story of our animation. We had a different version of it on our minds when we started working on it. But the characters behaved horribly when we forced them to follow a rigid story. It now feels almost deliberate that the brush wouldn't go where we wanted it to. It led us all the way.

So relax if things aren't taking shape the way you expected them to. Have fun. Lose yourself and follow your hand instead.
