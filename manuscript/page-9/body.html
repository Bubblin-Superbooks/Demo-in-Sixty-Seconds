Melody

A nice melodious piece of music works like a potion on the senses. It sets the mood for the viewer. Spend some time to search the right piece for your animation. Keep at least 2-3 tracks at hand so that you can ditch and fallback to another in on the last moment.

In our animation we wanted a warm retro-feel because our app was all about handwritten electronic letters. We shortlisted a few compositions with a comfortable feel and finally chose Parisian Waltz for our video.

Actually, we dug a little deeper than that. It made sense to use a song on a more commonly heard Ionian mode or the diatonic major scale. The Ionian mode has a happy-go-easy feel to it and is known to make the listeners feel happy. The slow paced measure, the simple G-C-D and Am chords and a simple merry-go-round pattern made Parisian Waltz a good choice for us to work with.
