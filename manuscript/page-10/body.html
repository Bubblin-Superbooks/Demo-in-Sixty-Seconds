Music is the soul

Good music is crucial. It is the soul of your video. Unless you decide to use a voice-over, music will give an ambience to the story of your animation. Also, it will be much tolerable during animation-making when the same piece is to be listened to over and over again.

Here are some sites for royalty-free backing tracks:

  1. Soundbible
  2. Purple Planet
  3. Free Sound
  4. SoundJay

Watch out for copyrights and IP. Use music that is either royalty-free or distributed under Creative Commons or is Public Domain.

Lastly, always credit the artist.
