Music is the soul

Good music is crucial. It is the soul of your animation.

Unless you decide to use a voice-over, music will impart ambience to the story of your animation. Also it will be much tolerable during animation making when you have to listen to it over and over again.

Here are some sites for royalty-free backing tracks:

  1. Soundbible
  2. Purple Planet
  3. Free Sound
  4. SoundJay

Look out for copyrights and protections. Use music that is either royalty-free or distributed under Creative Commons or is Public Domain.

Always credit the artist where credit is due.
