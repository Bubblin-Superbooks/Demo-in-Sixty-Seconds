Introduction

It is a chicken and egg problem when you're starting out on a new video. Should the story be frozen first or one could start with something else -- something that you're good at already.

Professionals try to freeze the story first. But that's the difference -- they're professionals. And can draw up characters and direct the scene to match the storyline.

In your case this approach will not work.

Why so? Because terrible drawing and animation skills! Zero experience in direction and poor visibility on piecing out scenes correctly, one after the other to map the whole story.
