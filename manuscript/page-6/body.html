Chicken or egg?

It is always a chicken or egg problem when starting out on a new animation project. Should the story come first or the characters its key scenes?

Professional artists freeze the story first. But there's the key difference -- they're professionals. And can draw characters and scenes and direct their hands to follow a storyline.

In our case this approach did not work.

Why so? Because terrible drawing and animation skills! Zero experience in direction and poor visibility on piecing out scenes correctly, one after the other to map the whole story.
