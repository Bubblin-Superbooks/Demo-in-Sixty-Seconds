Panning & Immersion

Remember we have only the first few moments, 5 or 10 seconds at best, to impress and hook the viewer. Panning and immersion is a great way to do that.

Observe this animation by Claude Medori, for example:

It uses panning to immerse the viewer into the vastness of an evil black ocean. You'll find this kind og camera panning is used in almost all Hollywood films where you, as the viewer, are placed into the story, location and situation through a surreal motion of the camera. Secret sauce!
