Technology

Most of our sketching & artwork was done using plain old paper & pencil. Later we scanned up the artwork and retraced the outlines using a cheap Wacom Pentablet. It cost us $99, the only dollar we spent for this video.

For animation we used Adobe Flash CS5 but sadly Flash is dying. There is ToonBoom if you're looking for an alternative. We also used Adobe After Effects for coloring and finishing touches.

Let's not go into a rigorous tutorial on how to use Flash CS5 in this book but there is a nice tutorial on YouTube that we relied on heavily.
