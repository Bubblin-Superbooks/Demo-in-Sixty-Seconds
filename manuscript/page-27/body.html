Steps that work

Broadly speaking, these are the steps that we followed:

  1. Find good music first, lock the length of the video
  2. Sketch multiple characters after your ideal customer
  3. Think up a simple and fun customer story
  4. Reference reality for artwork and scenes
  5. Follow your hand, see what style works best for you
  6. Write a simple script for your animation
  7. Storyboard: draw keyframes that are 5 seconds apart
  8. Split animation into 3 parts: Intro, Outro and Mid-story
  9. Setup a new project on Adobe Flash CS or ToonBoom
  10. Port music into the project first
  11. Place keyframes on the timeline
  12. Fill in the animation in-between keyframes.
  13. Complete the entire story without too much detail
  14. Remain flexible about the story
  15. Add color to your animation and polish the scenes.
  16. Iterate over it adding new details each time
  17. Composit your video, balance colors, hue & saturation
  18. Publish online, preferably on Vimeo
  19. Embed the video on your site.
  20. Share and spread the word
