Long or short?

Short. Ask anyone, short is sexy.

Misogyny aside, it is important to keep the length of your video short. In fact, keep it the shortest possible. 30 seconds or a minute. 90 seconds at the most.

We decided to keep the length of our animation at 60 seconds exactly.
