Music first

Choose music first.

It will help you freeze the length of the animation from the very beginning. And push you to optimize everything else i.e. story, actions, scenes and motion within that time-frame.

It's like what Twitter does to people with its 140 characters limit. Remember, for every extra second added to your animation there will be at least 24 additional frames of work to do. You certainly don't want to lose all your energy before finishing the project.
