Choose music first.

We did that too. It will help you freeze the length of the animation from the very beginning. And push you to optimize everything else i.e. story, actions, scenes and motion within that time-frame.

It's like what Twitter does to people with its 140 characters limitation. Impose a limit. You certainly don't want to lose all your energy before finishing the animation. Especially when there are 18 other things that can also kill your startup.

Remember, for every extra second added to your project there will be at least 24 additional frames of more animation to do.
