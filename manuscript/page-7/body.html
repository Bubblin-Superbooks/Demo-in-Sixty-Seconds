Music first

Take our advice, choose music first.

It will help you freeze the length of the animation. And push you to optimize everything else i.e. story, actions, scenes and motion rate within that limit.

This is like Twitter, how it pushes people to optimize and write their messages within 140 characters limit. Remember, for every extra second added to your animation there will be at least 24 additional frames of work to do. You don't want to lose all your energy before finishing the project.
