Preface

Before you dive into animation-making for a demo of your startup product here's our first advice:

Don't do it.

Even though we were able to reach a meaningful end with our little excursion into hand-made animation in the hindsight it feels the decision was reckless. Reckless especially when we hadn't drawn an apple since the first grade.

No really, avoid if you can.

Animation is hard. Really hard. It's a complete no-go if you've been a hacker or manager all your life. Do a screencast instead or find an artist who can do it for you in your budget.
