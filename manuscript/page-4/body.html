Preface

Before we dive into animation-making for your product here's our quintessential piece of advice:

Don't do classical animation by yourself.

Yes. Don't do it if you've been a hacker or manager all your life. Try a screencast instead or find an artist who can do it for you.

Here's a little fact about our excursion into hand-made animation: In the hindsight we concluded that it was a reckless decision. Especially, because we hadn't drawn an apple since our first grade.

We really mean this. Sincerely.
