Forewarning

Before you dive into making animation:

Though we did reach a meaningful end with our little excursion into hand-made animation in the hindsight it feels reckless. Reckless for having taken it up in the first place, especially when we hadn't drawn an apple since the first grade. Avoid it if you can.

Animation is really hard. If you've been a hacker or a manager all your life we recommend you to do a screencasting instead or find an artist who can do this for you for your budget.

** Animation is hard **
